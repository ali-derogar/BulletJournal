Debugging Tips

Open browser console (F12) and look for:

  • "GoalForm rendered with:" - shows what period is passed to form
  • "handlePeriodSelect called with:" - shows selected period
  • "loadAllGoals: Starting to load goals for selected periods:" - shows what periods are being queried
  • "Querying with filters:" - shows database query parameters
  • "Query completed, found goals:" - shows how many goals were found
